# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a friendly and respectful experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior

- Be respectful and kind to others  
- Give helpful feedback  
- Welcome new contributors  
- Use clear and polite language  

### Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or hate speech  
- Trolling or insulting comments  
- Spam or irrelevant contributions  
- Disrespectful behavior  

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For any issues, please open an issue in this repository.
